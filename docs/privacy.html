KPI Matrix Grid — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026

Summary

KPI Matrix Grid is a Power BI custom visual. It runs entirely inside your Power BI report and does not collect, store, transmit, or share any data of any kind.

What the visual does with your data

The visual renders whatever fields and measures you bind to it in Power BI — KPI values, goals, conditional formatting codes, period names, and related formatting settings — entirely within your browser or Power BI Desktop session. This rendering happens locally on your own device.

The visual does not:

  • Make any network requests, to Baztec or anyone else
  • Send your report data anywhere outside your own Power BI session
  • Store any data outside of Power BI's own report file
  • Use cookies, local storage, or any browser storage mechanism
  • Use analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind
  • Require sign-in, accounts, or authentication of any kind

Permissions

The visual requests no special capabilities from Power BI. It does not request web access, local storage access, or any other privilege beyond rendering the data already present in your report.

Format pane settings

Formatting choices you make in the Power BI format pane (colors, fonts, column widths, and similar) are saved by Power BI itself, inside your own report file — the same way they are for any built-in Power BI visual. Baztec has no access to these settings or to your report.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the update will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
